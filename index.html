About Regina

Regina grew up and went to college in Ohio where she spent her time reading fantasy books, running with friends, and carousing with family.

About Devin

Devin grew up and went to college in Minnesota where he spent time outside enjoying the beautiful rolling scenery of the midwest. He also spent time inside exploring the beautiful synthetic scenery of various video games.

About Lucy

Lucy enjoys the warmth of sunlight, laps, and unattended laptops. As the first pet of the househould she is the pet boss, despite being the smallest.

About Gimli

Gimli enjoys the quiet solitude of places where Lucy and (especially) Wink won't bother him. Once, he ate an entire catnip plant. He spent the weekend hiding in the closet, but he recovered and has been doing well in narcotics anonymous.

About Wink

Wink enjoys chewing on sticks, chasing his own tail, and "playing" with Gimli. His friendly demeanor and icy blue eyes won our hearts when we failed our first experiment as foster pet owners.
